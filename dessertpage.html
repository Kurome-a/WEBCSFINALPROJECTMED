Apple Enchilada

A Mexican dessert, featuring cinnamon-spiced apple filling wrapped in a flour tortilla

0 min

Ingredients

  • 21 ounce apple pie filling
  • 6 (8 inch) flour tortillas
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup packed brown sugar
  • ½ cup water
  • ⅓ cup margarine

Process (Step-by-step)

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F 5 min
  2. Spoon fruit evenly onto all tortillas and sprinkle with cinnamon 3 min
  3. Roll up tortillas and place seam side down on a lightly greased baking pan. 3 min
  4. Bring white sugar, brown sugar, water, and margarine to a boil in a medium saucepan. Reduce heat and simmer, stirring constantly 3 min
  5. Pour sauce evenly over tortillas 1 min
  6. Bake in the preheated oven 20 min

Chocolate Éclair Cake

A no-bake pudding dessert that's so quick and easy to make

0 min

Ingredients

  • 16 graham crackers
  • 3 cups cold milk
  • 6 ounces instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 8 ounces whipped cream,
  • 16 ounces chocolate

Process (Step-by-step)

  1. Line the bottom of the pan with a single layer of graham crackers. 2 min
  2. Mix whipped cream into pudding mix until combined. 5 min
  3. Spread 1/2 of the pudding mixture over graham cracker layer. 2 min
  4. Top with another layer of graham crackers. Cover with remaining pudding. Top with a final layer of graham crackers. 2 min
  5. Frost the top layer with chocolate 2 min
  6. Refrigerate, before serving 120 min

Chocolate Mint Brownies

A multi-layered dessert that combines a fudgy chocolate brownie base with a refreshing, creamy mint

0 min

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 ½ cups chocolate syrup
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups confectioners' sugar
  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • 2 tablespoons creme de menthe liqueur
  • 6 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Process (Step-by-step)

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F 5 min
  2. Grease your baking dish 2 min
  3. In a large bowl, cream together 1 cup sugar and 1/2 cup of softened butter until smooth. 3 min
  4. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in the chocolate syrup. Stir in the flour until just blended 5 min
  5. Spread the batter evenly into the prepared pan and bake in the preheated oven, or until top springs back when lightly touched. Cool completely in the pan. 30 min
  6. In a small bowl, beat the confectioners' sugar, 1/2 cup butter or margarine and creme de menthe until smooth 3 min
  7. Spread evenly over the cooled brownies, then chill until set. 5 min
  8. In a small bowl over simmering water, or in the microwave, melt the remaining 6 tablespoons of butter and the chocolate chips, stirring occasionally until smooth. 3 min
  9. Allow to cool slightly, then spread over the top of the mint layer. Cover, and chill before serving 60 min

Chocolate Peanut Butter Ice Cream Sandwich

A frozen dessert that combines chocolate, peanut butter, and ice cream

0 min

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 14 ouncesweetened condensed milk
  • ¾ cup extra crunchy peanut butter
  • 9 frozen ice cream sandwiches
  • 12 ounce hot fudge
  • ½ cup cocktail peanuts

Process (Step-by-step)

  1. Beat cream and vanilla together in a large bowl with an electric mixer on medium speed until stiff peaks form 10 min
  2. beat in sweetened condensed milk and peanut butter on low speed until smooth 5 min
  3. Arrange ice cream sandwiches in a baking dish 3 min
  4. spread peanut butter mixture evenly over ice cream sandwich layer 3 min
  5. Cover the dish with plastic wrap; freeze until firm 300 min
  6. Cut into squares. Spread hot fudge over top; sprinkle with peanuts, to serve 3 min

Crème Brûlée

A French dessert consisting of a custard topped with a layer caramelized sugar

0 min

Ingredients

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar

Process (Step-by-step)

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F 5 min
  2. Whisk together cream and 3 tablespoons sugar in a microwave-safe bowl until combined 2 min
  3. whisk again to dissolve sugar. Whisk in egg yolks and vanilla until smooth 2 min
  4. Pour cream mixture into ramekins on top of a roasting pan. Pour enough hot water into the pan to reach halfway up the sides of the ramekins 3 min
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until custard is set but slightly jiggly in the center when shaken 50 min
  6. Remove ramekins from hot water and chill in the refrigerator until cold 120 min
  7. Sprinkle 1 tablespoon sugar evenly over the top of each dessert 1 min
  8. Use a kitchen torch to melt and lightly toast sugar topping until brown and bubbly 1 min
  9. Let sugar topping cool to harden before serving. 2 min
Times per step are totaled for you.

Crêpes

A French pancake made very thin made from a simple batter of flour, eggs, milk, and melted butter

0 min

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 ⅓ cups milk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt

Process (Step-by-step)

  1. Whisk together eggs, milk, flour, melted butter, sugar, and salt in a large bowl until smooth 3 min
  2. Heat a medium skillet or crêpe pan over medium heat. Grease the pan with a small amount of butter or oil applied with a brush or paper towel. 2 min
  3. Using a serving spoon or small ladle, spoon about 3 tablespoons crêpe batter onto the hot pan, tilting the pan to evenly coat the bottom surface 1 min
  4. Cook until golden brown 2 min
  5. Serve warm with topping of your choice and enjoy 1 min

Lemon-Ricotta Cake

An Italian-inspired cake featuring citrus and ricotta cheese

0 min

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cake flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • ¼ cup milk

Process (Step-by-step)

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F 5 min
  2. Grease an 8-inch round springform pan with butter and line with parchment paper 2 min
  3. Mix flour and baking powder together in a small bowl. 1 min
  4. Cream sugar and softened butter together in a large bowl with an electric mixer. 3 min
  5. Add vanilla and beat 1 min
  6. Add eggs, one at a time, mixing thoroughly after each addition 5 min
  7. Add in flour mixture; beat until combined 2 min
  8. Add ricotta cheese, lemon zest, and lemon juice to batter; beat on medium speed 2 min
  9. Add milk and beat on low speed only until combined 2 min
  10. Pour batter into the prepared baking pan and bake in the preheated oven 50 min
  11. Let cake cool completely before serving 5 min
Times per step are totaled for you.

Contains gluten, dairy, cheese, lemon
