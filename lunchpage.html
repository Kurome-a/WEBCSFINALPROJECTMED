Mediterranean Lentil Soup

A nourishing meal that is often vegan, high in fiber and protein

0 min

Ingredients

  • 8 cups chicken stock
  • 1 cup diced carrots
  • 1 cup green lentils, rinsed
  • 1/2 cup jarred marinated artichokes, drained
  • 1/2 cup diced celery
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable bouillon paste
  • 3 sprigs fresh oregano
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cups escarole
  • 1 lemon
  • Salt & pepper

Process (Step-by-step)

  1. Place the chicken stock, carrots, lentils, artichokes, celery, sun-dried tomatoes and tablespoon of their oil, bouillon paste, oregano, lentils, and onion in a slow cooker. 2 min
  2. Stir in the escarole, lemon zest and juice and let it sit 10 min
  3. Season with salt and pepper and serve with crusty bread alongside. 1 min
