Cheesy Garlic Omelette

Fluffy 3-egg omelette with cheddar, herbs, and a hint of garlic.

0 min

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp shredded cheddar
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Chopped chives (optional)

Process (Step-by-step)

  1. Beat eggs with salt and pepper. 2 min
  2. Melt butter in a nonstick pan; sauté garlic briefly. 1 min
  3. Pour eggs, cook until just set; add cheddar. 3 min
  4. Fold omelette; rest and garnish with chives. 1 min

Classic Pancakes

Light, fluffy pancakes with maple syrup.

0 min

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp butter (melted)
  • Pinch of salt

Process (Step-by-step)

  1. Whisk dry ingredients; add wet and mix until just combined. 5 min
  2. Preheat pan and lightly butter. 2 min
  3. Cook pancakes in batches (flip once when bubbles form). 6 min
