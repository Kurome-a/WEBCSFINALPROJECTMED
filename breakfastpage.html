Cheesy Garlic Omelette

Fluffy omelette with cheddar, herbs, and a hint of garlic.

0 min

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tbsp shredded cheddar
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Chopped chives (optional)

Process (Step-by-step)

  1. Beat eggs with salt and pepper. 3 min
  2. Melt butter in a nonstick pan; sauté garlic briefly. 2 min
  3. Pour eggs, cook until just set; add cheddar. 5 min
  4. Fold omelette; rest and garnish with chives. 1 min

Acai Breakfast Bowl

Made with the frozen and mashed fruit of the açaí palm

0 min

Ingredients

  • Two 4-ounce packets unsweetened frozen acai puree
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 3 tablespoons granola
  • 2 tablespoons pomegranate seeds
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened coconut flakes

Process (Step-by-step)

  1. Break the frozen acai up a little by slapping the sealed packets on the countertop or hitting them with a meat mallet. 2 min
  2. Blend the berries with 1/2 the banana, 1/4 cup of the blueberries and the honey in the blender 2 min
  3. When it's consistency is like a thick smoothie; transfer to a cereal bowl. 1 min
  4. Slice the remaining 1/2 banana. Arrange the slices, the remaining 1/4 cup blueberries, granola, pomegranate seeds and coconut flakes in neat piles or rows on top of the acai. 2 min

Contains acai, banana, blueberries, polmegranate seeds, coconut

Corned Beef Hash

Hearty dish made from chopped corned beef, potatoes, and onions

0 min

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 8 ounces cooked corned beef, diced
  • 1 white onion, finely chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 2 cups potatoes, peeled/shredded
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 large eggs
  • Pinch of salt and ground pepper
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese

Process (Step-by-step)

  1. Heat the oil in a medium skillet over high heat 3 min
  2. Add the corned beef and cook, stirring until it releases some fat and browns slightly 3 min
  3. Stir in the onion, bell pepper and potatoes and cook, undisturbed, until brown and crisp on the bottom 6 min
  4. Continue cooking, turning the hash as it browns evenly 15 min
  5. Place the cheese slices on top of the hash, reduce the heat and let sit until the cheese melts 1 min
  6. heat the butter in a nonstick skillet over medium-high heat 3 min
  7. Fry the eggs and season with salt and pepper. 5 min
  8. Top each portion of hash with a fried egg. 1 min
Times per step are totaled for you.

Contains eggs, beef, onions

Total time: 0 min

Breakfast Burritos

hand-held meal consisting a variety of hot breakfast fillings wrapped around a flour tortilla

0 min

Ingredients

  • 2 medium tomatillos
  • 1 jalapeno pepper
  • 1 small red onion, quartered
  • 2 cups roughly chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 lime
  • 4 ounces dried chorizo, thinly sliced
  • 3 1/2 cups diced brown potatoes
  • 4 eggs
  • 16-ounce can beans
  • 4 10-inch flour tortillas, warmed
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • salt and ground pepper

Process (Step-by-step)

  1. Preheat the broiler 5 min
  2. Put the tomatillos, jalapeno and red onion on a baking sheet and broil, until tender and charred in spots 10 min
  3. Transfer the vegetables to a blender and add the cilantro, lime juice, 1/4 cup water, 3/4 teaspoon salt and a few grinds of pepper; puree until smooth. 5 min
  4. Cook the chorizo in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally, until crisp, 2 min
  5. Add the potatoes to the drippings in the skillet; cover and cook, stirring halfway through, until golden brown and warmed through 10 min
  6. Add to the bowl with the chorizo and stir. 2 min
  7. Add the eggs to the skillet and cook over medium heat, stirring, until scrambled 2 min
  8. Transfer to a bowl and cover. Wipe out the skillet and add the beans; warm through 2 min
  9. Season with salt and pepper. Spread the beans in the center of each tortilla and top with the potatoes, chorizo, eggs and cheese. 5 min
  10. Fold the bottom edge of the tortilla over the filling, then fold in the sides and roll up. Serve with the tomatillo salsa. 5 min
Times per step are totaled for you.

4

Contains chorizo, cheese, jalapeno

Total time: 0 min

Breakfast Casserole

Oven-baked dish made with eggs, cheese, and various other breakfast foods

0 min

Ingredients

  • 10 tablespoons salted butter
  • 8 onion rolls
  • 1/4 cup sliced roasted red peppers
  • 8 ounces cream cheese
  • 1 cup grated mozzarella
  • 2 cups milk
  • 3 tablespoons basil pesto
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 10 eggs

Process (Step-by-step)

  1. Generously butter a 9-by-13-inch baking dish 5 min
  2. Tear the onion rolls into chunks and place them in the bottom of the baking dish. 2 min
  3. Sprinkle the roasted peppers on top, then tear the cream cheese over the peppers. 2 min
  4. Sprinkle with the mozzarella, then cut the butter into pats and scatter over the top. 3 min
  5. Mix together the milk, pesto, parsley, oregano, salt, pepper and eggs in a large bowl and pour slowly over the top of the dish, making sure to get it all around and into the crevices. 5 min
  6. Wrap tightly with plastic wrap, then foil and refrigerate 30 min
  7. To cook from frozen: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. 5 min
  8. Remove the foil and plastic wrap, then re-cover the casserole with the foil and Bake 45 min
  9. Remove the foil and continue baking until firm and set, 10 min
