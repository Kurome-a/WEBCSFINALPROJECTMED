Acai Breakfast Bowl

Made with the frozen and mashed fruit of the açaí palm

0 min

Ingredients

  • Two 4-ounce packets unsweetened frozen acai puree
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 3 tablespoons granola
  • 2 tablespoons pomegranate seeds
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened coconut flakes

Process (Step-by-step)

  1. Break the frozen acai up a little by slapping the sealed packets on the countertop or hitting them with a meat mallet. 2 min
  2. Blend the berries with 1/2 the banana, 1/4 cup of the blueberries and the honey in the blender 2 min
  3. When it's consistency is like a thick smoothie; transfer to a cereal bowl. 1 min
  4. Slice the remaining 1/2 banana. Arrange the slices, the remaining 1/4 cup blueberries, granola, pomegranate seeds and coconut flakes in neat piles or rows on top of the acai. 2 min

Avocado Toasts

Popular for being nutritious and customizable, frequently enjoyed for breakfast

0 min

Ingredients

  • One ripe avocado, halved, pitted and peeled
  • 4 slices whole grain or whole wheat bread
  • 1 clove garlic, peeled and halved
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil or unsalted butter, softened
  • Salt & pepper
  • Crushed red pepper flakes (optional)

Process (Step-by-step)

  1. Mash the avocado with a fork in a shallow bowl until chunky. 3 min
  2. Season with fine salt and black pepper. 2 min
  3. Toast the bread until browned and crisp. 3 min
  4. Lightly rub 1 side of each slice with the cut side of the garlic until fragrant 2 min
  5. discard the garlic. Lightly brush the toasts with oil, and season with fine salt and pepper. 1 min

Breakfast Burritos

Hand-held meal of breakfast fillings wrapped around a flour tortilla

0 min

Ingredients

  • 2 medium tomatillos
  • 1 jalapeno pepper
  • 1 small red onion, quartered
  • 2 cups roughly chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 lime
  • 4 ounces dried chorizo, thinly sliced
  • 3 1/2 cups diced brown potatoes
  • 4 eggs
  • 16-ounce can beans
  • 4 10-inch flour tortillas, warmed
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • salt and ground pepper

Process (Step-by-step)

  1. Preheat the broiler 5 min
  2. Put the tomatillos, jalapeno and red onion on a baking sheet and broil, until tender and charred in spots 10 min
  3. Transfer the vegetables to a blender and add the cilantro, lime juice, 1/4 cup water, 3/4 teaspoon salt and a few grinds of pepper; puree until smooth. 5 min
  4. Cook the chorizo in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally, until crisp, 2 min
  5. Add the potatoes to the drippings in the skillet; cover and cook, stirring halfway through, until golden brown and warmed through 10 min
  6. Add to the bowl with the chorizo and stir. 2 min
  7. Add the eggs to the skillet and cook over medium heat, stirring, until scrambled 2 min
  8. Transfer to a bowl and cover. Wipe out the skillet and add the beans; warm through 2 min
  9. Season with salt and pepper. Spread the beans in the center of each tortilla and top with the potatoes, chorizo, eggs and cheese. 5 min
  10. Fold the bottom edge of the tortilla over the filling, then fold in the sides and roll up. Serve with the tomatillo salsa. 5 min
Times per step are totaled for you.

Breakfast Casserole

Oven-baked dish made with eggs, cheese, and various other breakfast foods

0 min

Ingredients

  • 10 tablespoons salted butter
  • 8 onion rolls
  • 1/4 cup sliced roasted red peppers
  • 8 ounces cream cheese
  • 1 cup grated mozzarella
  • 2 cups milk
  • 3 tablespoons basil pesto
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 10 eggs

Process (Step-by-step)

  1. Generously butter a 9-by-13-inch baking dish 5 min
  2. Tear the onion rolls into chunks and place them in the bottom of the baking dish. 2 min
  3. Sprinkle the roasted peppers on top, then tear the cream cheese over the peppers. 2 min
  4. Sprinkle with the mozzarella, then cut the butter into pats and scatter over the top. 3 min
  5. Mix together the milk, pesto, parsley, oregano, salt, pepper and eggs in a large bowl and pour slowly over the top of the dish, making sure to get it all around and into the crevices. 5 min
  6. Wrap tightly with plastic wrap, then foil and refrigerate 30 min
  7. To cook from frozen: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. 5 min
  8. Remove the foil and plastic wrap, then re-cover the casserole with the foil and Bake 45 min
  9. Remove the foil and continue baking until firm and set, 10 min

Farmer's Breakfast

Protein-rich meal designed to provide energy for a day of hard, physical labor

0 min

Ingredients

  • 6 tablespoons butter
  • 2 slices ham
  • 3 pieces sausage
  • 3 slices bacon
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 pounds potatoes
  • 1/4 cup olive oil

Process (Step-by-step)

  1. In a large nonstick skillet, melt 1 tablespoon of the butter over medium-high heat. 2 min
  2. Brush the ham lightly with honey, then fry it until the texture is firm 3 min
  3. Fry the sausage and bacon together 15 min
  4. Pour out the excess grease but do not wipe out the skillet. 2 min
  5. Add the second tablespoon of butter to the skillet and heat until melted 3 min
  6. Crack the eggs into the skillet and fry for 2 minutes, sprinkling on some salt and pepper. Flip the eggs and cook them for another 45 seconds. 3 min
  7. Bring salted water to a boil in a medium pot. 5 min
  8. Add the potatoes and boil until tender 15 min
  9. Drain and allow to dry thoroughly, then cut into wedges. 3 min
  10. Heat the olive oil with the butter over medium-high heat in a skillet 3 min
  11. Add the potatoes, sprinkle on some salt and pepper and pan-fry until golden brown and crisp 15 min
  12. Serve on a plate 2 min
Times per step are totaled for you.

Cheese Danish

Breakfast pastry consisting of a flaky, buttery dough with sweetened cream cheese filling

0 min

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces cream cheese, at room temperature
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 2 tablespoons ricotta cheese
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1/3 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon grated lemon zest
  • 2 sheets puff pastry
  • 1 egg beaten with 1 tablespoon water

Process (Step-by-step)

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F. Line a sheet pan with parchment paper. 10 min
  2. Place the cream cheese and sugar in the bowl of an electric mixer fitted with a paddle attachment and cream them together on low speed until smooth 10 min
  3. Put the mixer on low, add the egg yolks, ricotta, vanilla, salt, and lemon zest and mix until just combined. 4 min
  4. Unfold 1 sheet of puff pastry onto a lightly floured board and roll it slightly with a floured rolling pin until it's a 10 by 10-inch square 5 min
  5. Roll out each sheet of puff pastry on a lightly floured surface to a 12-inch square. Cut each sheet into 4 equal squares. 15 min
  6. Cut the sheet into quarters with a sharp knife 5 min
  7. Place a heaping tablespoon of cheese filling into the middle of each of the 4 squares 3 min
  8. Brush the border of each pastry with egg wash and fold 2 opposite corners to the center, brushing and overlapping the corners of each pastry so they firmly stick together 5 min
  9. Brush the top of the pastries with egg wash. 3 min
  10. Roll out each sheet of puff pastry on a lightly floured surface to a 12-inch square. Cut each sheet into 4 equal squares. 15 min
  11. Place the pastries on the prepared sheet pan. Repeat with the second sheet of puff pastry and refrigerate the filled Danish 15 min
  12. Bake the pastries, rotating the pan once during baking, until puffed and brown 20 min

Corned Beef Hash

Hearty dish made from chopped corned beef, potatoes, and onions

0 min

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 8 ounces cooked corned beef, diced
  • 1 white onion, finely chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 2 cups potatoes, peeled/shredded
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 large eggs
  • Pinch of salt and ground pepper
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese

Process (Step-by-step)

  1. Heat the oil in a medium skillet over high heat 3 min
  2. Add the corned beef and cook, stirring until it releases some fat and browns slightly 3 min
  3. Stir in the onion, bell pepper and potatoes and cook, undisturbed, until brown and crisp on the bottom 6 min
  4. Continue cooking, turning the hash as it browns evenly 15 min
  5. Place the cheese slices on top of the hash, reduce the heat and let sit until the cheese melts 1 min
  6. heat the butter in a nonstick skillet over medium-high heat 3 min
  7. Fry the eggs and season with salt and pepper. 5 min
  8. Top each portion of hash with a fried egg. 1 min
Times per step are totaled for you.

Lentil Frittata

Egg-based dish similar to an open-faced omelet or a crustless quiche, with cooked lentils

0 min

Ingredients

  • 1 cup green lentils
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 potato, diced very small
  • 2 cups spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 chopped kalamata and green olives
  • 1 chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 sliced tomato
  • 10 eggs
  • Salt & pepper

Process (Step-by-step)

  1. Cook the lentils with 1 teaspoon salt and 1/2 teaspoon black pepper with 2 cups of water in a medium saucepan over medium-high heat until fully cooked but not mushy 30 min
  2. Drain lentils of excess water in a fine sieve and let them come to room temperature. 3 min
  3. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees 10 min
  4. Sauté the onion, stirring frequently, until soft and translucent 10 min
  5. Add in the garlic and finely diced potato and sauté, stirring often, until the potatoes start to soften 15 min
  6. Add the spinach and cook, tossing, until wilted but still vibrant green 5 min
  7. Add the cooked lentils and 1 teaspoon salt and stir. 3 min

  9. Pour over the vegetables in the skillet and gently stir to combine 3 min
  10. Then top with the rest of the feta and bake until the eggs are set 25 min
  11. Top with some chopped olives and parsley, tomato slices and a good drizzle of olive oil. 2 min
