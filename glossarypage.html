Glossary

Quick reference for ingredients & dishes.

Quinoa

A gluten-free seed cooked like a grain; complete protein.

Aioli

Garlic-forward emulsion similar to mayo, used as a sauce.

Ganache

Chocolate + cream mixture used for frostings and truffles.

Poach

Gentle cooking in barely simmering liquid.

Roux

Flour and fat cooked together; thickens sauces and soups.

Caramel

Sugar heated until browned; base for sweets and sauces.
